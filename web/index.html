About the Author

"You succeed when you help others along the journey!"

Adora Eden originally from the Republic of Congo in central Africa grew up with a desire to help others by giving time, money, and talents. As a ditinguished software engineer with many patents, Adora dedicates the majority of her time to empower the younger generation to be productive while navigating career, family, passion, dreams, and goals.
